LEARN & EARN PROGRAM

CodersTrust Platform


CodersTrust is an international company that is changing the way freelancers learn and earn. They have the knowledge to make you better as a computer programmer and the ability to promote you on freelance portals through personalized learning paths that increases your skills and earnings on freelance portals.


CodersTrust are equipped with an innovative Learn & Earn path or best known in blocks which comprise of coding, soft skill and freelancing. Each block starts with the foundations of any given subject, and ends with completing a hands-on project which will be assessed by mentors all around the world.
