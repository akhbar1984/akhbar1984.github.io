ABOUT US

The Laparoscopic Gynaecology Surgery and Hands-On Animal Workshop is now in its fifth year running; providing continuous in-depth review of the know-how, latest information and techniques in Gynaecologic Laparoscopic Surgery.


Our affiliation with The asia Pasific Association of Gynaecologic Endoscopy & Minimally Invasive Therapy (APAGE) continues while we welcome the newly formed Gynaecological Endoscopic Society of Malaysia (GESM). GESM provides the long overdue yet essential niche for our Malaysian gynaecologists with special interest in Endoscopy. Our collaboration with GESM will further establish University Malaya as a go-to centre for Gynaecological Endoscopic training and education. We await excitedly for future collaborations, reaching out to all via this national platform.


While we have always promised continous education, we also remember our initial dream of establishing a recognised and certified training programme in the future. We have projected the possibility by creating a Masterclass Series. Participant who are already competent surgeons will benefit through the refresher course point of view, whilst new, keen and budding surgeons may apply and be guided closely via a set of our teachings [didactic lectures, live surgery & video series, hands-on session] and progressively identified, later selected to be followed up through individual preceptorship (mentor-mentee arrangement).

Course Description

This year we are going back to basics with focus on Total Laparoscopic Hysterectomy. Our featured faculty will take us back to the essential core knowledge required and progressively build up with demonstrations whilst sharing tips and tricks gathered over years of experience on the "bread and butter" of Gynae Endoscopy: TLH

The Animal Hands-On will focus of Total Laparoscopic Hysterectomy, How-to-Handle Complications and troubleshooting ideas etc.

The Perioperative Nursing Programme continues for the benefit of nurses and endoscopic operating theatre staff; featuring mini lectures, breakout sessions and case discussions.


Target Audience

Gynaecologists strongly motivated to adopt or continue minimal invasive procedures and to be exposed to the latest clinical techniques through the invited USA faculty and Malaysia faculty.

O&G physicians, Gynaecologic surgeons, OBGYN fellows, residents, postgraduate students, endoscopic operating theatre staff who would like to pursue training particularly progressing from Intermediary to advance Level.


Program Objectives

Specifically designed to be executed together along with the panel of invited faculty while learning tips and techniques of enchancing personal surgical skills as well as continue and develop training programs in respective institutions (if any).
